# ShepherdAI ✝️🤖  
Guiding Conversations with Faith  

## 📖 Project Description  
**ShepherdAI** is an AI-powered Christian chatbot designed to **guide, encourage, and inspire believers** while also sharing the message of Christ with non-believers.  

Built with natural language processing, ShepherdAI can:  
- Answer questions about the **Bible**  
- Provide **daily devotionals**  
- Offer **prayer support**  
- Engage in uplifting conversations that strengthen faith  

By combining technology and spirituality, ShepherdAI acts as a digital **shepherd**, bringing comfort, truth, and wisdom wherever users are.  

---

## ✨ Features  
- **Biblical Guidance** – Instant answers to questions about scripture and Christian living  
- **Prayer Support** – Request prayers or be guided in personal prayers  
- **Daily Devotionals** – Encouraging Bible verses and reflections  
- **Evangelism Tool** – Share responses to help spread the Gospel  
- **Friendly Conversations** – Faith-filled and uplifting dialogue  

---

## 🎯 Target Audience  
- Christians seeking encouragement, Bible study help, or prayer guidance  
- Non-believers curious about Christianity  
- Churches, ministries, and individuals looking for a digital tool for outreach  

---

## 🌍 Vision  
To make God’s Word **accessible and relatable** in the digital era, helping millions strengthen their faith and encounter Christ through technology.
